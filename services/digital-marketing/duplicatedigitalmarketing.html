SEO AUDIT

Under SEO Audit, our SEO expert will do a deep-dive of existing SEO strategy if applicable. Apart from this our expert will evaluate your website based on various aspects. It includes factors influencing the website ranking on search engine, website performance on online/offline mode and presence of website from SEO prospective. We, Digitroops perform the SEO audit and accordingly plan the SEO strategy best suited for your business.

KEYWORD RESEARCH

The power of keyboard research lies in better understanding of target market and how they are searching for your content, services or products. It is crucial step in which we understand your business and your goals as well as do a competitor analysis on SEO prospective.
  Based on SEO audit and keyword research, we discover keywords in line with your business goals to drive qualified traffic to the website.

CONTENT BUILDING

A website loaded with high-quality content of interest of site users will give them a reason to stay and a reason to come back as users came to site to find information. Also quality content in line of your business will translate directly into the ranking of the site for related keywords.

CONTINUAL IMPROVEMENT

CUSTOMIZED STRATEGY

A customized strategy is a personalized approach for your business which helps in increasing number of website visits, sales leads, calls, emails etc. Through this strategy, we plan best suited strategy which enables our clients to increase their ROI.

ON-PAGE OPTIMIZATION

Following high quality content, we tackle on-page optimization. In this stage, we perform multiple checks to improve on-page aspects of the website. These checks are categorized as technical optimization(Server speed, Source code, IP addresses), content(Text, Structural text elements, Graphics, Videos, Meta-tags), Internal links and structure (Logical structure and crawl depth, Internal linking, Canonization, URL structure, Focus) and design (Mobile optimization, File sizes, Call-to-Action).

REPORT

Frequently Asked Questions

Which platform is best for eCommerce web development for my project?

Well, the choice of the platform for eCommerce web development majorly depends on factors like your business objectives and aims, design options, scalability, integrations, pricing, and payments, etc. Also, your business size is a deciding factor to choose the right eCommerce development platform.

If you are unsure about the platform choice for your business, get in touch with us with your requirements. Our experts will suggest the best and the most optimized solution for your online business.

How much does it cost to build an eCommerce website?

Well, the overall cost to build an eCommerce website varies from agency to agency. Also, the cost of an e-commerce project highly depends on your business needs and requirements. eCommerce web development cost is determined by the following factors like design, functionality, development, integration, support and maintenance, company selection and marketing and SEO.

To get the right quote for customized eCommerce solutions, you can get in touch with our eCommerce development experts.

Is there any limit on the size of the product or customer database?

No, as such there are no limits on the size. The biggest benefit of having an online store is that you can add unlimited products and catalogs and at the same time you can grow your customer base as you require.

What type of developer hiring models do you offer?

Dedicated hiring - Need a team for ongoing work under your control for a large project? Dedicated hiring is best for you. Pay as you go monthly.

Time & material-based hiring - If your project scope is not defined, go for Time & Materials-based model. Get developers for the hours you need, based on your requirement.

Fixed price model - If your project scope is defined, you can go for a fixed pricing model. Hire a complete team of people you need and pay in milestones.

Can I hire more resources in the later stages of the projects?

Yes. We would love to accommodate you with the right pool of eCommerce development experts. Hire the best eCommerce developers based on your project requirements.

Is testing included in the pricing structure?

Testing is something any good developer performs before submitting their work. And if you want, you can also hire Quality analysts or test engineers for better testing.

I want to hire eCommerce web development experts. So, how are we going to work this out?

We can start by discussing and finalizing your project requirements. Then after a quick documentation, you can hire dedicated eCommerce developers or a team from an eCommerce website development company for your project.

Am I going to own all the source codes by the offshore team?

All the project work built by your offshore team is considered as your intellectual property rights. So, yes, all source codes created by your offshore team is owned by you.

How to choose the right eCommerce development agency for my requirements?

First, you should note that eCommerce web development is not a short-term game. It involves a long process of maintenance and constant updates. So, make sure to hire an eCommerce web development agency that can take care of your every requirement including development, deployment, support, and maintenance.

Also, choose a company that has prior experience in building eCommerce websites. Make sure to talk directly with the development experts so that you know they have understood what they have to do for your business.
