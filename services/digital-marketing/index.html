Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digital Marketing?

Digital Marketing means using the internet to reach your target customers. Businesses leverage digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and other websites to connect with potential customers for their business growth.

What is the role of digital marketing?

Digital Marketing helps you to generate new traffic, leads, and sales for your business by reaching people who are interested in your products and services. With the right marketing strategy, Digital Marketing provides a higher Return On Investment (ROI) than traditional marketing.

How will digital marketing help my business?

Reaching the right people is the ultimate goal of any marketing campaign. Digital Marketing allows you to reach your potential customers at the right time through various tactics. One of the beautiful features of Digital marketing strategies is you can define your audience, the ads you will run, and what time to run them, etc. Unlike traditional marketing, every digital marketing campaign is measurable.

What is the difference between digital marketing and digital advertising?

Digital marketing allows businesses to raise brand awareness, share information about products, engage with the audience, and monitor online reputation. Digital advertising allows businesses to advertise and promote products, offer discounts or specials, and attract buying customers. Simply put, digital marketing establishes a target consumer audience while digital advertising drives this audience to sales.
